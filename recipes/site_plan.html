Simple Recipes Site Plan

Luke Robison

WDD 131-A1

Overview

Purpose

To provide simple and easy recipes to cooking beginners

Audience

This website will help Beginners who are new to cooking, Busy students, and People who prefer short instructions and easy ingredients.

Dynamic elements

Elements include: Navigation Toggle, Random Recipe Generator, Recipe List Rendering, Filtering System, Data Import, and a Search Bar
